Step & Synchro

Event Details

Get ready to find your mate to whirl and twirl to the fine tunes of music!
Summit 2019 presents the expressive chance to partner up and groove to the Step & Synchro contest, brought to you by College of Engineering Chengannur. Don't miss out on this opportunity to pair up and sync your steps to the world!
Join us on 29, 30, 31 March 2019, leap to your favourite beats and find yourself winning prizes worth ₹9k!

Date: 29 March 2019
Registration fee: ₹300 per team

    Rules and Regulations

  1. Any number of teams can participate from a college but each team can contain only 2 members.
  2. No one is allowed to participate in more than one team.
  3. Time limit is a minimum of 4 minutes and maximum is 6 minutes.
  4. Use of property is allowed, but any kind of dangerous materials (sharp objects/fluid/flame) are strictly prohibited on the stage.
  5. Each team should register their entries along with the team members atleast 1 hour before the event.
  6. All members in the team must produce their valid college ID cards for their entries.
  7. Non possession of ID card by any team member will automatically lead to his/her disqualification in the competition.
  8. Team should submit their audio in mp3 format (USB) before the event starts.
  9. Any type of damage to the sound system and stage arrangements will not be entertained.
  10. Vulgarity/obscenity in any form is strictly prohibited. Any violations of the above mentioned rules will lead to disqualification.
  11. Decision of the Judges will be final.
  12. Registration is separate for boys and girls.
