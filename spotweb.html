    Rules and Regulations

  1. Participants can register either as an individual or a team of two.
  2. The teams will have to design an attractive and user-friendly website, that should be appealing and informative.
  3. Participants will be provided 2 hours to develop the website.
  4. The topic for the website will be given on the spot.
  5. Internet facility will be provided.
  6. Use of frameworks and pre-designed templates are not allowed.
  7. Participants can bring their own laptops if they wish. They may use HTML and CSS. They can also use JavaScript to make it more dynamic.
  8. Registrations can be done at venue.
  9. Decision of judges will be final.
