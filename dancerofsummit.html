Dancer of Summit

Event Details

Summit 2019 hosted by College of Engineering Chengannur is here with a huge shout out to all those dancers with their mesmerizing moves! Presenting the Dancer of Summit competition for those amazing dancers on 29th March 2019 as part of this unmatched extravaganza.

Let your feet fly to the beat and you stand a chance to win the prize money worth 7k!

Judging Criteria

The competition will have 2 rounds.
  1. First round:
    Spot Choreography round with mixed songs.

  2. B) Second round :
    Participant has to qualify the first round; Participant has to perform a well prepared choreo. (Minimum time is 3 minutes and maximum time is 5 minutes).

Rules

  1. Each participant should register their entries at least 1 hour before the event.
  2. Use of properties are allowed, but any kind of dangerous materials (sharp objects/fluid/flame) are strictly prohibited on stage.
  3. All the participants must produce their valid college ID cards for their entries.
  4. Non possession of ID card by any participant will automatically lead to his/her disqualification in the competition.
  5. All the participants should submit their audio in mp3 format (USB) before the event starts.
  6. Any type of damage to the sound system and stage arrangements will not be entertained.
  7. Vulgarity / obscenity in any form is strictly prohibited.
  8. Any violations of the above mentioned rules will lead to disqualification.
  9. Judges decision will be final.
  10. The committee has the complete authority regarding the competition.
