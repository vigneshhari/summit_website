About
Summit is the biennial techno-cultural fest conducted by College of Engineering, Chengannur, aimed on providing a platform for improving the technical, non-technical and cultural skills of the young engineering minds. It offers a plethora of events ranging from seminars and workshops on the state of art technology to contests that push student’s talents and brain beyond the limits, thereby fetching the best brains from engineering institutions across the nation. The maiden version of the event took place way back in 1998. Then onwards, the event had its sequels in 2000, 2002, 2005, 2009, 2012, 2014 and 2017, each time marking a better success record. The event has fast emerged as one of the best technical festivals of the country over the past years and now resounds as a familiar name across the colleges as well as the industry, nationwide. Today, SUMMIT is a wholesome platform that students across India can look up to.
